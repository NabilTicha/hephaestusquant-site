Quantitative research infrastructure for people who want to do this properly.

Small group. Factor models, regime detection, risk decomposition. We build the backtesting and analysis tools ourselves. Everything is open-source.

Research

Factor work

We study what drives returns, how those drivers decay, and when they stop working. Most of our current time goes into finding reasons to reject hypotheses.

Infrastructure

Kernel

Our Python backtesting and research engine. Modular, no vendor lock-in, designed for factor research specifically. Still in early development — see current status.

Process

Risk-first

We think about what can go wrong before we think about what might go right. Capital preservation over returns.

Active research

What we're working on

  • Factor · Equities Fama-French UMD momentum in STOXX Europe Mid 200 after realistic transaction costs (~40bps round-trip), 2005–2024.
  • Regime · Correlation Testing whether cross-asset correlation breakdowns cluster near VIX regime transitions. Using DCC-GARCH on G10 equity indices.
  • Crowding · Factor decay Modeling value factor decay as a function of short interest concentration — fitting to post-2015 US large-cap data.
