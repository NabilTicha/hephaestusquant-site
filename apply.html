Membership

Open problems.

We do not have a traditional application. Pick a problem. Submit a structured solution proposal. If the thinking is rigorous, we reach out.

Process

How it works

  1. Review the open problems below — research or engineering.
  2. Choose one that matches your interest and capability.
  3. Write a structured proposal: problem framing, methodology, expected output, limitations.
  4. Submit via the contact method at the bottom of this page.
  5. We review proposals on a rolling basis. If the work demonstrates rigor, we will reach out.

We are not looking for finished systems. We are looking for clear thinking, honest problem framing, and an understanding of what you do not know.

What we evaluate

Submission criteria

Problem understanding

Can you articulate why the problem is hard? What are the failure modes? Where does naive intuition break down?

Methodology

Is the proposed approach falsifiable? What would a negative result look like? What are the statistical assumptions?

Scope awareness

Does the proposal define clear boundaries? What is explicitly out of scope? Is the timeline realistic?

Intellectual honesty

Are limitations stated upfront? Is uncertainty acknowledged? We value a precise "I don't know" over a confident guess.
