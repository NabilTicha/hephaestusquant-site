Overview

What we are

Hephaestus Quant is a small, selective research group focused on systematic quantitative analysis. We build research infrastructure, develop methodology for factor-based investing, and study market structure through a falsification-first lens.

We operate at TU Delft. The group was founded in 2025 with a deliberate emphasis on depth over breadth — a small core working on hard problems with high standards.

What we are not

We are not a social club. We do not run stock-picking competitions or pitch sessions. There is no portfolio managed by committee and no consensus-driven investment process.

We are not a fund. We do not manage capital collectively. Members apply research independently to their own decisions. Hephaestus Quant produces methodology and infrastructure — not investment advice.
